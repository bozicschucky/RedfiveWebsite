Book Your Dinner Show

These days the importance of eating healthy, preparing food correctly and most importantly the perfect way to cook a meal, are ideals on which most people agree. How you cook food is just as important as what you cook.

We promote good health by running healthy cooking demonstrations either in family homes or in our own office Kitchen. We show how to prepare and cook food in a way that retains up to 93% of the valuable nutrition in the food, how to avoid cooking with oils and fats, and how to use very little water and heat which greatly increases the chances of vitamin and mineral absorption into the body.
