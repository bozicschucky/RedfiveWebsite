The Dinner Show

The concept behind the Dinner Show is to allow you to personally experience for yourself, in the comfort of your own home, surrounded by family and friends just how well the Red 5/Saladmaster system works.

Along with an informative presentation about important Health and Nutrition issues, guests will receive a cooking demonstration, all guests will have a delicious and tasty five course meal prepared and cooked before their eyes in around 35 minutes.

FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q.

Are we required to buy anything?

There is no pressure to purchase any of our products, all we ask is the chance to demonstrate their quality and excellence. We do not spend any money on advertising, we feel it's better to use any marketing budget to reward our customers and consultants, and the best way to do that is to run free absolutely no obligation in home dinners to demonstrate the many benefits of our system.

Q.

Do I need to supply food for the dinner show?

No! We supply all the food required for the dinner show :)

Q.

Do you cater for food allergies?

The Red5 consultant will call to discuss any food allergies before they book the dinner show with you.

Q.

How long does the dinner show take?

The dinner show last 2 and a half hours from start to finish including you eating the food. Red 5 consultants are only paid for that time if they are at your house any longer than that it is by mutual agreement after the show.

Q.

How many people can attend the dinner show?

We recommend that up to 2 families attend the dinner show

Q.

What equipment do I need to supply?

Red 5 will supply most of the equipment, however we are going to need the following:

  1. mixing bowl for the cake
  2. a couple of large plates
  3. 2 clear glasses
  4. a couple of forks
  5. a sharp knife
  6. access to a hand washing area

Q.

Why would you give away a free meal?

We do not spend any money on advertising, we feel it's better to use any marketing budget to reward our customers and consultants, and the best way to do that is to run free absolutely no obligation in home dinners to demonstrate the many benefits of our system. Advertising is very expensive and in some cases in can be prohibitively expensive. We realized many years ago that the most effective form of advertising is personal recommendations from happy clients. You do not need to purchase anything and there is no obligation to purchase anything.
All we ask is that if you are impressed with what you see that you will happily refer us onto your friends and relatives.
